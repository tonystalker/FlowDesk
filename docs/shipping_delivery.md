# Shipping and Delivery Guide

## Shipping Methods

FlowDesk offers several shipping options to meet your needs:

| Method | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|--------|-------------------|------|
| Standard Shipping | 5–7 business days | Free on orders over $50 |
| Express Shipping | 2–3 business days | $14.99 |
| Overnight Shipping | Next business day | $29.99 |
| International Standard | 10–15 business days | Varies by destination |
| International Express | 5–7 business days | Varies by destination |

## Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed after 3 PM EST are processed the following business day. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order ships.

## Tracking Your Order

To track your order:
1. Log in to your FlowDesk account at app.flowdesk.com
2. Navigate to **Orders** → **Order History**
3. Click on the relevant order number
4. The tracking number and carrier link will be displayed under **Shipment Details**

Alternatively, use the tracking number from your shipping confirmation email directly on the carrier's website (FedEx, UPS, or USPS).

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Received
If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received the package:
- Check with neighbors or building management
- Look in a safe location (porch, garage, mailroom)
- Wait 24 hours — carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered prematurely
- If still missing after 48 hours, contact support@flowdesk.com with your order number

### Damaged Package
If your package arrives damaged:
1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and contents
2. Do not discard the packaging
3. Contact support@flowdesk.com within 48 hours of delivery
4. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost

## Shipping Restrictions

- We do not ship to P.O. boxes for orders containing hardware
- Hazardous materials (e.g., certain battery packs) may have shipping restrictions
- Some international destinations may incur customs duties or import taxes — these are the buyer's responsibility
