# Product Warranty Information

## Standard Warranty Coverage

All FlowDesk hardware products come with a standard 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase. This warranty covers:

- Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
- Hardware malfunctions under normal use conditions
- Battery defects (for portable devices) — batteries are warranted to retain at least 80% of original capacity for 1 year

## What Is Not Covered

The warranty does not cover:

- Damage caused by accidents, misuse, or unauthorized modifications
- Normal wear and tear (e.g., scratches, fading, minor cosmetic imperfections)
- Damage caused by third-party software, accessories, or peripherals
- Water or liquid damage (unless the product is rated as water-resistant)
- Products with removed or altered serial numbers
- Damage caused by operating the product outside its specified environmental conditions (temperature, humidity)

## Extended Warranty

## Filing a Warranty Claim

1. Contact support@flowdesk.com or call 1-800-FLOWDESK
2. Provide your order number and product serial number
3. Describe the issue in detail — photos or videos of the defect are helpful
4. Our support team will diagnose the issue and provide next steps within 2 business days
5. If a replacement or repair is approved:
   - We will send a prepaid shipping label for the defective product
   - Repairs are completed within 7–10 business days
   - Replacements are shipped within 3–5 business days

- Bug fixes and security patches for the current version and one prior major version
- 90-day money-back guarantee if the software fails to perform as described in the product documentation
