# Billing and Payment Methods

## Accepted Payment Methods

FlowDesk accepts the following payment methods:

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Bank Transfer / ACH**: Available for Enterprise plans (minimum $500/month)
- **PayPal**: Available for Starter and Professional plans
- **Wire Transfer**: Available for annual Enterprise contracts
- **Purchase Orders**: Accepted for Enterprise customers with approved credit

## Billing Cycle

- **Monthly billing**: Charged on the same date each month (e.g., if you subscribed on March 15, you'll be billed on the 15th of each month)
- **Annual billing**: Charged once per year — annual plans receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing
- **Prorated charges**: When adding seats mid-cycle, you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing period

## Viewing Your Invoices

1. Log in to your FlowDesk account
2. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Invoice History**
3. Download invoices as PDF for your records
4. Invoices are also emailed to the billing contact on record

## Updating Payment Information

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Payment Method**
2. Click **Update Payment Method**
3. Enter the new card details or select an alternative payment method
4. Click **Save** — the new method will be used for the next billing cycle

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. You will receive an email notification with the reason (e.g., expired card, insufficient funds)
2. You have a 7-day grace period to update your payment method
3. During the grace period, your account remains fully functional
4. If payment is not resolved within 7 days, your account will be downgraded to read-only mode
5. After 30 days of non-payment, your account may be suspended — contact billing@flowdesk.com to resolve

## Cancellation and Refunds

- You can cancel your subscription at any time via **Settings** → **Billing** → **Cancel Subscription**
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access until then
- Annual plans: cancellations within the first 30 days are eligible for a prorated refund; after 30 days, no refund is issued
- For refund requests, contact billing@flowdesk.com

## Enterprise Billing

Enterprise customers receive:
- Dedicated billing contact and custom invoicing
- Net-30 payment terms (Net-60 available on request)
- Volume discounts for 100+ seats
- Custom contract terms negotiated with your account executive
