# Contact and Escalation Procedures

## Support Channels

| Channel | Availability | Best For |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Email (support@flowdesk.com) | 24/7 (response within 24 hours) | Non-urgent issues, detailed inquiries |
| Live Chat (app.flowdesk.com) | Mon–Fri, 8 AM–8 PM EST | Quick questions, real-time troubleshooting |
| Phone (1-800-FLOWDESK) | Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM EST | Urgent issues, account emergencies |
| Community Forum (community.flowdesk.com) | 24/7 (community-moderated) | How-to questions, feature discussions |
| Twitter/X (@FlowDeskSupport) | Mon–Fri, 9 AM–5 PM EST | Quick status updates, public inquiries |

## Priority Levels

| Priority | Description | Target Response Time | Target Resolution Time |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| P1 — Critical | Platform is down, data loss, security breach | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| P2 — High | Major feature broken, significant impact on workflow | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| P3 — Medium | Minor feature issue, workaround available | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| P4 — Low | Cosmetic issue, feature request, general inquiry | 24 hours | Best effort |

## Escalation Path

If your issue is not being resolved satisfactorily:

### Level 1 — Support Agent
- Initial contact via any support channel
- Agent will attempt to resolve using knowledge base and standard procedures
- Expected resolution: within target SLA for the issue's priority level

### Level 2 — Senior Support / Team Lead
- If Level 1 cannot resolve within SLA, the ticket is automatically escalated
- You can also request escalation by replying to the ticket with "Please escalate"
- Senior support has access to advanced diagnostic tools and can engage engineering

### Level 3 — Engineering Escalation
- For complex bugs, infrastructure issues, or data-related problems
- Engineering team is engaged directly — you may be contacted for additional diagnostic information
- Updates provided every 2 hours until resolved

### Level 4 — Management Escalation
- For unresolved critical issues or disputes about service quality
- Contact escalations@flowdesk.com or ask your account executive
- A support manager will take ownership and provide a resolution plan within 4 hours

## Enterprise Escalation

Enterprise customers with active contracts have additional escalation options:
- **Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM)**: Direct point of contact for all issues
- **Priority queue**: Enterprise tickets are prioritized above standard tickets
- **War room**: For P1 incidents, a dedicated Slack/Teams channel is created with all relevant stakeholders
- **Post-incident review**: After P1 resolution, a written Root Cause Analysis (RCA) is provided within 5 business days

## Filing a Complaint

If you wish to file a formal complaint about your support experience:
1. Email complaints@flowdesk.com with your ticket number(s) and a detailed description
2. Our Customer Experience team will review within 2 business days
3. You will receive a written response with any corrective actions taken
