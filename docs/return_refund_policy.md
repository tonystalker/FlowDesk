# Return and Refund Policy

## Overview

FlowDesk is committed to customer satisfaction. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may request a return or refund within the applicable return window.

## Return Window

- **Standard purchases**: You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.
- **Enterprise licenses**: Enterprise software licenses may be returned within 14 days of activation if fewer than 5 seats have been provisioned.
- **Promotional items**: Items purchased during promotional events (e.g., annual sale, Black Friday) are eligible for return within 15 days.

## Conditions for Return

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

1. The product must be in its original condition — unused, unmodified, and with all original packaging and documentation.
2. Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt) is required.
3. Digital products that have been downloaded and activated more than twice are not eligible for return.
4. Custom or personalized products cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective.

## Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification regarding the approval or rejection of your refund.

- **Approved refunds**: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited to the original payment method.
- **Partial refunds**: In some cases (e.g., items not in original condition, returned after 30 days), a partial refund may be granted at our discretion.
- **Shipping costs**: Original shipping costs are non-refundable. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

## Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange a product, contact support@flowdesk.com with your order number and a description of the issue.

## Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement for a full refund. After 2 hours, if the order has been processed for shipping, it cannot be cancelled and must follow the standard return process.

## Contact

For return and refund inquiries, reach out to our support team at support@flowdesk.com or call 1-800-FLOWDESK (1-800-356-9337), Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST.
