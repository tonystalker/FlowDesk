# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is FlowDesk?
FlowDesk is a customer support platform that helps teams manage support requests across email, chat, social media, and phone channels. It includes ticket management, a knowledge base, analytics, and AI-powered features to help your team resolve issues faster.

### Is FlowDesk available in my country?
FlowDesk is available globally. Our platform supports 12 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified), Chinese (Traditional), and Arabic.

### Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! All new accounts receive a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can subscribe to any plan or continue with the free Starter limitations.

## Account & Billing

### How do I change my plan?
Go to Settings → Billing → Current Plan → Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately (prorated). Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

### Can I get a refund?
Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time but are not refundable for the current period. Annual plans are refundable within the first 30 days (prorated). See our Return and Refund Policy for full details.

### What happens if my payment fails?
You have a 7-day grace period to update your payment method. During this time, your account remains fully functional. After 7 days, your account enters read-only mode. After 30 days, it may be suspended.

## Features

### Does FlowDesk support multiple languages?
Yes. The platform interface is available in 12 languages. Additionally, our AI features can detect the language of incoming tickets and route them to agents with matching language skills.

### Can I customize the customer-facing chat widget?
Yes. Go to Settings → Channels → Chat Widget → Customize. You can change colors, logo, greeting message, operating hours, and pre-chat form fields. Enterprise customers can fully white-label the widget.

### Does FlowDesk integrate with Slack?
Yes. The Slack integration allows you to:
- Receive notifications for new tickets and updates in a designated Slack channel
- Reply to tickets directly from Slack
- Create tickets from Slack messages using the /flowdesk command
- Set up alert rules for specific ticket conditions

### Can I use FlowDesk with my existing CRM?
Yes. FlowDesk integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive. The integration syncs customer data, displays CRM context alongside tickets, and logs interactions back to the CRM.

## Technical

### Is my data encrypted?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). See our Data Privacy and GDPR page for full details.

### Where is my data stored?
Data is stored in Google Cloud Platform data centers. US customers: Iowa data center. EU customers: Belgium data center (data sovereignty guaranteed — data never leaves the EU).

### What is the API rate limit?
Professional plans: 100 requests per minute. Enterprise plans: custom limits (default 500 req/min). See the API Integration Guide for details.

### Does FlowDesk support SSO?
SSO via SAML 2.0 is available on Enterprise plans. Supported identity providers include Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, and OneLogin. See Account Management for setup instructions.

### Can I export my data?
Yes. Go to Settings → Data Export to download your tickets, conversations, analytics, and user data in JSON or CSV format. Enterprise customers also have access to the Data Export API for programmatic exports.

## Troubleshooting

### The mobile app is slow — what can I do?
Close background apps, ensure you have at least 500 MB free storage, check your internet connection (minimum 1 Mbps), and update to the latest app version. See our Mobile App Troubleshooting guide for detailed steps.

### I can't log in — what should I do?
First, try resetting your password via the Forgot Password link. If you use SSO, tap "Sign in with SSO" and enter your organization's domain. If 2FA codes aren't working, check that your authenticator app's time is synced. See Password Reset and Security for more help.

### My emails aren't being received by FlowDesk — what's wrong?
Check that your email forwarding is configured correctly in Settings → Channels → Email. Verify that the FlowDesk forwarding address is correct and that your email provider isn't blocking our servers. Add `support@flowdesk.com` and `*.flowdesk.com` to your allowlist.
